THE 'BLACK PEARL' LOOT

Long time ago, there was a pirate ship called ''BLACK PEARL'' that went on travelling seven seas, stealing and looting gold and silver.

Like his daily routine, Jack Sparrow had gone fishing in the 'Adventure Sea' after having dinner and bidding adieu to his sweet wife. The fishing season is not going good for many days and Jack is sad because of that. But today is different because today you are going to accompany Jack in his night venture...

Earlier in the day 'Black Pearl' had sunk and its treasures are floating on the ocean surface untouched. Jack thanks God and hurries to collect the treasures. But he has to escape from the coastal guards.

But fishing is Jack's wine and he can't leave fishing and go hunting the treasures. You as Jack's friend show your robotic skills and make a manual bot which would collect the floating treasures one by one avoiding any obstacles(if any) on the ocean surface and deposit them on the surrounding land. Help Jack and collect as many treasures as you can in the least time because with increase in time there is increasing amount of risk in the treasures getting looted. Then it would be a great weekend with baked prawn and roasted tuna and a glass of Jack Daniel!!!
